# Cutzy - Enterprise Link Management

Cutzy is a secure, scalable, and powerful URL shortening service designed for modern teams.
It provides advanced analytics, custom domains, and team collaboration features.

## Core Features

- **Link Management**: Shorten, track, and manage URLs easily.
- **Analytics**: Detailed insights into click data, including location, device, and referrer.
- **Custom Domains**: Use your own brand domains for links.
- **QR Codes**: Generate QR codes for any short link.
- **Team Collaboration**: Invite members and manage permissions.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: Next.js 16 (App Router), React, Tailwind CSS
- **Backend**: NestJS (Microservices architecture)
- **Database**: PostgreSQL with Prisma ORM
- **Authentication**: JWT, OAuth (Google, GitHub)
